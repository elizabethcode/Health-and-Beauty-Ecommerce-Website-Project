About Us
Established in 1997

Healthful Core is an international health and wellness store that has opened more than 20 stores in Europe. We supply customers with a wide range of products ranging from health suppliments, vitamins, specialist foods and natural beauty products. With over 30 years of experience in the health and retail industry our brand has become more visible in different market places in different countries such as China, India, United States of America, Indonesia, Pakistan and Brazil.
